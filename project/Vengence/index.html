Story:

You are in a top secret research facility where human experiments were made in order to create real psychic humans.
You are one of these research subjects.
You died and came back from the dead in order to take your revenge on the executives that run the shady bussiness that killed you

Mechanics

Around the facility, researchers are present everywhere.
These researchers can exorcise you if you give them enough time, so you need to strike first.
Use your powers to attack them, but be weary about the cooldown this attack has.
Your goal is to wipe out all the executives of the company spread around the laboratory. Once you have done that, you can finally move on
